# 🛡️ RakshaMarg Safety Guide - Simple Version

## For anyone using this app (written for ease of understanding)

---

## **STEP 1: Add Your Trusted Contacts** 🤝

Before you travel, add people you trust (family, friends, police):

1. Go to **"Check Route" page**
2. Click **"Add Trusted Contacts"** or **"Manage Contacts"** button
3. Type contact name and phone number
4. Save each contact

**Why?** These people will be notified if you press the SOS button.

---

## **STEP 2: Check Your Route** 🗺️

When you want to travel:

1. Enter where you're starting from ("From")
2. Enter where you're going ("To")
3. Click **"Check Route"**
4. The app shows you the safest path with a safety score

**What the colors mean:**
- 🟢 **Green (70+):** Safe route
- 🟡 **Yellow (50-70):** Some concern
- 🔴 **Red (below 50):** High risk area

---

## **STEP 3: Enable Safety Heartbeat** 💓

This is your automatic safety alarm:

**Click: "Enable Safety Heartbeat"**

What happens:
- Every 2 minutes, the app checks on you
- You'll get a small notification asking "Are you safe?"
- Tap "Yes, I'm fine" when prompted
- If you don't respond twice, we send emergency alert to your contacts

**This is like a heartbeat monitor** - if it stops, help comes automatically.

---

## **STEP 4: If You Feel Unsafe - Press SOS** 🆘

**THE BIG RED SOS BUTTON**

When you feel in danger:
1. Press the large red **SOS button**
2. Your phone vibrates/alerts
3. Your trusted contacts get a message immediately
4. They see your exact location
5. They can call you or come help

**That's it. ONE TAP = Your contacts know you need help.**

---

## **STEP 5: When Help Arrives** ✅

When a trusted contact gets your alert:
- They receive your location
- They can tap "I'm coming" 
- The app updates to show "Help is on the way ✓"
- You'll see green confirmation on your screen

---

## **SIMPLE RULES**

### ✓ DO:
- ✅ Always add 2-3 trusted contacts before traveling
- ✅ Enable Safety Heartbeat for night travels
- ✅ Keep your phone charged
- ✅ Keep your phone in your hand or pocket
- ✅ Trust your gut feeling - if something feels wrong, press SOS

### ✗ DON'T:
- ❌ Don't ignore "Are you safe?" notifications - tap Yes/No
- ❌ Don't give your app password to anyone
- ❌ Don't wait if you feel unsafe - press SOS immediately

## **FAQ**

**Q: What if my phone has no internet?**
A: The app won't work. Try to reach a place with WiFi or phone signal.

**Q: What if I press SOS by mistake?**
A: Quickly tell your contacts it was an accident. They'll understand.

**Q: Can my contacts see where I am all the time?**
A: No. Only when you press SOS or enable Safety Heartbeat.

**Q: What if I'm somewhere safe but the app says "High Risk"?**
A: Trust your feeling first. The app is just a guide.

**Q: Can I use this with my friends too?**
A: Yes! Share the app. Add their contact info and enable heartbeat.

**Remember: Your safety is #1 priority. When in doubt, always ask for help.** 🛡️
